PHOOL MAKHANA KHEER:SHAHI KHEER-Dessert For Fast



What does it taste like?
     =>Sweet, soft and chewy, giving you perfect delight after a sumptuous meal. The addition of jaggery gives it a distinct aroma, making it even more delicious 
          to taste.
Ingredients:
          1 cup makhana
          2 cups milk
          powdered green cardamom
          cashews,raisins and pistachios
          sugar
          saffron
          ghee
Method of Preparation:

          A) Roasting makhane and cashews -
               1. Heat 2-3 tbsp ghee in pan andd add phool makhane and cashews.
               2. Roast makhane in ghee till they become crunchy and cashews turn golden.
               3. Put the roasted makhane and cashews in a plate and keep it aside.

          B) Making kheer -
               1. Heat 500ml of milk in a saucepan and stir at intervals. Let the milk boil.
               2. Whilst the milk is getting heated, reserve 1/3 cup makhane and grind the remaining. Add cardamom seeds along with a pinch of saffron strands.   
               3. Grind to fine powder. when the milk begins to boil, then add sugar as per taste.
               4. Add the powdered makhane and the 1/3 reserved cup. Stir well. 
               5. Simmer till the milk thickens a bit, for about 9-10 minutes on a low to medium flame.
               6. Scrape the evaporated milk solids and add the roasted cashews and raisins.
               7. Stir and simmer the kheer for a minute. Serve hot or chilled, as per taste with garnished pistachios.
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
    Cooking Time: 20 minutes
          Serves: 2-3
