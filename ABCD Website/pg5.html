RED VELVET CUPCAKE:ALL TIME FAVOURITE EGGLESS DESSERT



What does it taste like:
                   =>These Easy Eggless Red Velvet Cupcakes are tender, light, and moist and delicious all at once!! They are super easy to whip up for your next 
                     get-together. Perfect for crowded birthday parties and school celebrations.
Ingredients
                        A) Cupcakes:
                             1 cups (175 gram) self-rising flour
                             cup (26 gr) unsweetened cocoa powder
                             1 cup (200 gr) sugar
                             1/2 cup (113.4 gr) unsalted butter ,softened 
                             1 tablespoon (15 ml) vegetable or canola oil
                             1 teaspoon (5 ml) vanilla extract
                             1 cup (240 ml) whole milk 
                             1 tablespoon (5 ml) apple cider vinegar
                             2 teaspoons (10 ml) red food color 
                        B) Cream Cheese Frosting:
                             226 gram cream cheese (softened)
                             1 cup (113.4 gr) unsalted butter ,softened
                             Pinch of kosher salt
                             2 teaspoons (10 ml)pure vanilla extract
                             4 cups (560 gr) confectioners' sugar (more if needed according to taste)
Method of Preparation

                       A) Cupcakes:
                            1. Preheat oven to 350F. Line the cupcake pans with liners and lightly spray them with cooking spray.
                            2. Combine flour and cocoa powder in a bowl. Set aside.
                            3. In the bowl of your stand mixer fitted with paddle attachment mix beat together butter and sugar until smooth on medium speed. Once 
                                smooth, add oil and vanilla and mix until well combined. Add milk and vinegar. Add the food coloring and keep mixing until well combined.
                            4. Add the flour mixture; mix just until combined. Please do not overmix.
                            5. Divide batter evenly among liners, about three-quarters full each.
                            6. Bake for 18 - 22 minutes on the same shelf, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
                            7. Let it rest for 5 minutes in the pan then turn out onto a cooling rack and allow to cool completely before frosting.

                      B) Frosting:
                            1. Beat in a large bowl with an electric mixer the softened cream cheese and butter on medium speed for 3 - 4 minutes, scraping bowl 
                                occasionally, until smooth and creamy.
                            2. Stir in salt and vanilla, then stir in confectioners' sugar. Add more confectioners' sugar as needed until frosting is a thick spreadable 
                                consistency. If it's too thick you can add a 1 tablespoon of milk. If it's too thin you can add more confectioners' sugar.
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
    Cooking Time: 20 minutes
          Serves: 16 cupcakes
