MANGO HALWA: A Delicious Pulpy Dessert



What does it taste like?
      =>Soft and spongy, with the sweet pulpy taste of mango urging you to seek for more.
Ingredients:
          Mango Puree or Pulp 1.5 cup
          Cornflour / Cornstarch 1/2 cup
          Water 1/2 cup
          Sugar 3/4 cup
          Cardamom Powder / Yelakai Podi 2 pods crushed
          Nuts 1/4 cup (cashews, badam, walnuts, pista)
          Ghee 5 tblspn
Method of Preparation:
         1. Grease a pan in which you are going to set the halwa well with ghee and set aside.
         2. Now take cornflour in a bowl, add in water and mix well. 
         3. Now heat 2 tbsp ghee in a kadai, add in mango pulp and cook this for 3 to 4 mins.
         4. Now add in the cornflour mix and mix well. Add in sugar and keep mixing, it will thicken up.
         5. Now start adding 1 tblspn of ghee and keep cooking, it will turn glossy and at one stage it will start to leave the sides of the pan. 
         6. Add in cardamom powder and nuts. Mix well.
         7. Pour this into the pan and set aside to cool down for couple of hours
         8. Now cut it into squares and enjoy.
         9. Store in fridge and serve chilled.
Preparation Time : 10 mins
    Cooking Time : 20 to 25 mins
            Makes: 9 big pieces
